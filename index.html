GIS MS Student - UW Madison. Conservation Biologist

About

I am a graduate of Michigan State's Fisheries and Wildlife program specializing in Conservation Biology. I grew up in North Carolina and Michigan and like getting out and exploring the natural world both near and far. This combined with my time in and out of college led me to work towards the mapping side of conservation.

Experience

I have worked and served with various levels of government and non-profit oranizations, with the USDA, US Forest Service, CISMA, the Michigan DNR, Michigan State University, and Americorps. The tasks and training I undertook were varied ranging from extensive data collection in the field to its organization, analysis, and reporting. I learned various problem solving and bureaucratic navigation skills along with hardware and software operation and troubleshooting.
